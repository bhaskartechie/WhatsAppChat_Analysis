Generate chat file from WhatsApp

Open WhatsApp > Open your desired Group > tap More options(vertical three dots) > More > Export chat> Select WITHOUT MEDIA >Save to drive (or) Gmail (or) Share file to desktop > then upload exported chat file

Assumptions

  1. If you are added in the middle of the group conversation there may a chance to loose information of group creation info
  2. Single month created group sent messages over time will not be displayed
  3. If the word count of the sent message is less than 10 words its considered as typed message not a copied/forwarded message
  4. Emojies are counted for the only to typed messages
  5. * items are not 100% correct due to taken calculations but it is sure for 95 to 99%
